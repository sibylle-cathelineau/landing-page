About Us

This site is thought, designed and made by a team of volunteers, invested in humanitarian actions. We want to make people find easily how they can improve the world!

Two friends, 1000 projects, one life

We are two friends, gone three years ago in Jordania to help refugees from Irak.

Helene, who was successfully graduated from EM Lyon two years ago, started working on luxuary hotels. But after two years of hardwordking, stress and speed, she realized that she wanted to give a real sense to her life, to have a real impact hearth, to give all she was able to, in order to contribute to a greater world.


Sibylle, a IESEG student, went to Madrid to realize an internship and improve her CV. But four months later, she quitted her job, dispointed by the lack of human sense in the firm and went back to Paris to find an action full of sense.


It's finally in Montfermeil, in a school of Esperance Banlieue association,one hour by rer from Paris, that Helene and Sibylle found what they were looking for. Helene has beed hired as a profesor for elementary section for two years and Sibylle stayed two months in the school as a volunteer with children.


The problem the girls faced is that it was so difficult to find where to contribute, there are so many amazing projects but also so many fraudulous ones.
Where to go? with who? how many time? what frequence? How can i have some volunteers feedbacks?
To make this search of sense easier for all, we created this website, and referred all the projects after a meticulous analysis.
